About Me

I am a current BYU-Pathway Worldwide student pursuing a Software Development degree, with over 6 years of experience in customer service and a proven track record in leadership roles. My career has been built on dedication, consistency, honesty, and coaching skills, qualities that helped me earn promotions to Supervisor positions at both Xfinity and Humana. Throughout my career, I have supported accounts such as Xfinity (Technical Support), Lyft (Critical Response Line), and Humana (Customer Service), where I consistently delivered high-impact results, guided teams, and enhanced the customer experience. These experiences strengthened my problem-solving, communication, and technical skills, which I now aim to apply in the software development field. I am passionate about bridging my customer service expertise with technology, adding value to projects through empathy, attention to detail, and leadership. My goal is to contribute to the tech industry while continuing to learn and grow as a developer.
